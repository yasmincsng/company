About Amprius

Amprius Technologies is a leading manufacturer of high-energy and high-power lithium-ion batteries, producing the industry's highest-known energy density cells. The company's commercially available batteries deliver up to 450 Wh/kg and 1,150 Wh/L. Headquartered in Fremont, California, Amprius pioneers silicon nanowire anode technology and serves over 100 partners across various industries including electric aviation, EVs, and consumer electronics.

Leadership & Vision

Yi Cui

Founder & Professor at Stanford

"To revolutionize energy storage with silicon nanowire anode technology."

Learn More

Yi Cui founded Amprius Technologies in 2008 to commercialize silicon anodes for high energy density lithium-ion batteries. A Professor at Stanford University, his original concept developed into the first commercially produced lithium-ion battery with 100% silicon nanowire anode, achieving breakthrough performance approaching 500 Wh/kg over hundreds of cycles. His work bridges academic research and commercial energy storage solutions.

Powering Innovation

Amprius Technologies continues to push boundaries with its silicon anode battery platforms. The SiMaxx™ series offers industry-leading energy densities with three configurations: High Power (400 Wh/kg), High Energy (500 Wh/kg), and Balanced designs. The SiCore™ platform provides reliable performance across power and energy optimized versions. Recent innovations include a revolutionary battery pack developed with Tenergy that's 31% lighter than industry standards, and custom 450 Wh/kg cells for premier electric aviation companies. These advancements position Amprius at the forefront of energy storage for mobility applications.
