About Saipem

Saipem is a global leader in engineering, drilling, and construction services for the energy and infrastructure sectors. The company specializes in designing and executing complex projects, both offshore and onshore, with a strong commitment to sustainability and innovation. Headquartered in San Donato Milanese, Italy, Saipem operates in over 70 countries worldwide with more than 34,000 employees of 122 different nationalities.

Leadership & Vision

Enrico Mattei

Founder

"To transform our clients' strategies and projects into competitive and sustainable infrastructures, plants, and processes."

Learn More

Enrico Mattei, then head of Eni, envisioned creating a globally competitive industrial business by merging SAIP and Snam Montaggi, leading to the establishment of Saipem in 1957. His vision was to create an Italian company capable of competing on the global stage in the energy services sector, combining technical expertise with innovative approaches to energy infrastructure development.

Recent Innovations

Saipem continues to innovate in the energy sector, focusing on sustainable solutions and expanding its expertise in renewable energy projects. The company has transitioned the Saipem 7000 vessel to support renewable energy projects, including offshore wind farm installations. In 2024, Saipem secured major contracts in Qatar and Saudi Arabia, boosting new contracts to €6.4 billion. The upcoming merger with Subsea7 in 2025 will create Saipem7, positioning the company as a leader in global offshore energy services with enhanced capabilities in both traditional and renewable energy sectors.
