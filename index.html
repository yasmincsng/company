About Zenmov

A Japan-based company headquartered in Tokyo, with a subsidiary in Makati City, Philippines. Specializes in smart mobility platforms including autonomous buses and micro-transportation using electric vehicles (EVs). Partners with MC Metro Transport Operation, Inc. and NEDO for energy-efficient public transport systems. Committed to reducing traffic congestion and promoting low-carbon, sustainable urban mobility.

Driving the Future

Zenmov's Smart Mobility Operation Cloud (SMOC) is a comprehensive platform for managing autonomous vehicles, optimizing dispatch based on travel demand, and supporting real-time traffic data. The Primary Rapid Transit (PRT) is their flagship autonomous bus service operating in New Clark City. They also provide electric vehicles and smart infrastructure solutions including smart poles and charging stations. The SMOC Operator Application allows managers to monitor vehicle status and generate schedules using AI-driven demand forecasting, while the SMOC User Application enables passengers to check vehicle locations, purchase tickets, and request on-demand services.
